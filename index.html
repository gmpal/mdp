How to Play

Try to guess as many words as possible within 90 seconds.

  1. You'll see a secret password word
  2. Come up with one-word clues to help guess this word
  3. When ready, click "Guessed It!" to move to the next word
  4. If stuck, click "Skip" to move to a new word (costs 2 points)

Each correct guess is worth 5 points!
